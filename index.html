Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate?

ImersAthon is open to all students (from Northeastern and other universities), creatives, technologists, and hobbyists passionate about XR. We welcome participants from all backgrounds and skill levels.

How much does it cost?

Thanks to our sponsors, we aim to make the event as accessible as possible. Stay tuned for details on registration fees. We will provide meals and resources throughout the event.

Do I need a team?

Not at all! We'll have dedicated team formation sessions on Day 1 where you can meet other participants and form a team.

What hardware is available?

We are actively working with sponsors to provide access to a range of VR headsets and AR glasses. However, we encourage you to bring your own equipment if you have it.
